Admission Process

We welcome new students to join our vibrant learning community at Shree Vidhya Mandir, Sindhari. Admissions are open for all grades, subject to seat availability and eligibility criteria.

Admissions Open: Academic Session 2025-26

How to Apply

  1. Obtain the application form from the school office or download it from our website.
  2. Fill in the required details and attach necessary documents (birth certificate, previous mark sheets, etc.).
  3. Submit the completed application form at the school office.
  4. Attend the entrance test/interview (if applicable).
  5. Wait for the admission confirmation from the school administration.
Required Documents:
- Birth Certificate
- Previous Academic Records
- Passport-size Photographs
- Address Proof
