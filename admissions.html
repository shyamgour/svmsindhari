Admission Process


Academic Session 2025-26
  1. Collect the application from from the school office
  2. Fill in the required details and attach necessary documents (birth certificate, previous mark sheets, etc.).
  3. Submit the completed application form at the school office.
  4. Attend the entrance test/interview (if applicable).
  5. Wait for the admission confirmation from the school administration.
Required Documents:
- Birth Certificate
- Previous Academic Records
- Passport-size Photograph
- Address Proof-Aadhar Card
